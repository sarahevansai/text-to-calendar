How it works

Simple as texting

No app to download. No account for family members to create. Just text the number.

📱

Text naturally

Type events the way you'd say them: "dentist wed 3pm" or "soccer thursday 4-6pm"

🧠

AI parses it

Claude understands natural language. No rigid format required — it just gets it.

📅

Google Calendar syncs

Events appear instantly in your family's Google Calendar, viewable by everyone.

✅

Confirmation reply

You get a text back confirming exactly what was added — date, time, and title.

👨‍👩‍👧

Whole family

Add all your family members' phone numbers. Everyone can text in events.

🔓

Open source

Fork it, deploy your own, customize it. MIT licensed. Your data stays yours.

Setup

Up and running in 15 minutes

Four steps, then just start texting.

1

Connect Google Calendar

Sign in with Google to link your family calendar. We only request permission to add events.

2

Add family members

Enter the phone numbers of everyone who should be able to text in events.

3

Set up Twilio (~5 min)

Get a free Twilio account and phone number. Point the webhook at your app. Full instructions included.

4

Start texting

Text "dentist friday 2pm" to your number. Watch it appear on the calendar. Done.
